About 5X

Five X Records is a new record label that seeks to sign artists talented in the world of electronic music. We are a small label that was formed in December 2011. We currently have four artists signed to the Five X label. As we expand, we hope to cover a variety of electronic music genres such as dubstep, DnB, electro, trance, and more.

Please direct all inquiries to fivex@music.com.
